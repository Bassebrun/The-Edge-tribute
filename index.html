David Howell Evans

The Master of the Sonic Landscape

  • Born - August 8th 1961, Barking in the UK.
  • 1962 - The family moved to Dublin City,Ireland.
  • 1976 - David saw a note on a bulletin board at Mount Temple Comphrehensive High School. It was written by a boy named Larry Mullen Jr, who wanted to start a rock band.
  • 1976 - David joined the band that became Feedback and changed to The Hype and finally they settled on U2. He became known as The Edge.
  • 1977 - David got his first delay, an Electro-Harmonix unit.
"He started playing when he was 14. Once David got the guitar in his hands, he wouldn't put the blooming thing down. And we'd be saying, "Would you shush now a minute, we just want to hear the news." - Gwenda Evans on David's early dedication to the guitar.
